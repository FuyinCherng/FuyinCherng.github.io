Welcome to my personal page! I'm Fu-Yin Cherng.

I am currently pursue the Ph.D. degree in the department of computer science of national Chiao Tung University (NCTU), Hsinchu, Taiwan. I am supervised by professor Wen-Chieh (Steve) Lin, who leads graphic and perception lab in NCTU(GP Lab). My research topics include Human-Computer Interaction, neuroergonomics and learning analytics.
