Welcome! I'm Fu-Yin Cherng.

I'm a PhD candidate in the computer science department of National Chiao Tung University (NCTU), Taiwan. I'm supervised by professor Wen-Chieh (Steve) Lin, who leads Graphic and Perception lab in NCTU (GP Lab). I received BS degree in computer science from NCTU in 2013, and had been a doctoral research assistant in the school of computer and communication sciences of EPFL from 2016 to 2017. My research interests include Human-computer Interaction (HCI), Neuroergonomics, and Data Science.
